Projects

Notes Site

The Notes site is a user-friendly web application designed to provide students in our college with easy access to course-related notes and study materials. It features a clean and visually appealing interface with subject-wise sections, allowing students to navigate and find resources effortlessly. The homepage showcases subjects with a carousel slider. The site is mobile-responsive and can be accessed from various devices. Although not active currently, it has the potential* to integrate with a MySQL database for structured data management. Students can also find external resource links for further study materials. Overall, the Notes site aims to enhance the learning experience by offering a centralized platform for course resources.

Anime Review Site

The Anime Reviews site is a captivating web platform that offers insightful reviews and information about popular anime series. With a visually appealing interface, the site showcases an array of well-known anime titles, such as Naruto, One Piece, Bleach, Death Note, Jujutsu Kaisen, and Classroom of the Elite. Each review provides a brief overview of the series, its plot, and the main characters, allowing anime enthusiasts to make informed choices about what to watch next. The site aims to serve as a valuable resource for anime lovers, helping them explore and discover exciting new shows within the vast anime universe. Whether you're a seasoned anime fan or just starting your anime journey, the Anime Reviews site promises an enjoyable and informative experience.
